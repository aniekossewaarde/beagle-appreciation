Boone the amazing Beagle

'all beagles are awesome'

Boone is a beagle born in September of 2021. He is of the bigger variety and has a tricolored coat. His character is playfull, energetic and he is always hungry! Whenever he gets the chance he'll chew up all shoes in sight and doesn't mind munching on any houseplants either. Boone is cuddly, friendly and so very loud. Which all is totally normal for beagles!
